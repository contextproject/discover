    1. Welcome to Discover

      The place to discover new music. Take this tour to learn more about this page.

      (Press Escape to skip this tour or click on the 'Skip' button)

    2. Search bar

      Here you can put the URL of the song you want to listen to.

    3. Reload!!

      Load the song which you filled in, in the previous step.

    4. SoundCloud player

      This is the SoundCloud player where you can actually play the music, comment on the song and etc.

    5. Change the volume!

      Here you can change the volume of the song that is playing

    6. Preview a song

      This gives you a short 10 second preview of how the song sounds like.

    7. Next!

      Gives the next song or gives the next part of the current song.

    8. Previous

      Opposite of next.

    9. You want something else?

      Loads a random song from SoundCloud.

    10. Split the Mix!

      Splits a mix or a song in different parts to play separately.

    11. Advanced

      This menu will give you some extra feautures and options.

    12. Autoplay

      If you want to listen to music non-stop switch the button to 'on' and it will automaticly keep playing music.

      This can be the next track in the playlist or a recommended song based on your preferences.

    13. How many splits do you want?

      Fill in a number greater or equal than zero or keep it empty to search for the default amount of splits

    14. Account login

      Allows you to connect with you SoundCloud account

    15. Like!

      When you are connected with your account, then you are able to like the current song.

    16. Dislike!

      You can also dislike a song.

    17. Forgot something?

      You can retake this tour if you press this link
